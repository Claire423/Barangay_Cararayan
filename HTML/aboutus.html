About us

Barangay Cararayan

MISSION

To initiate programs, project and activities with manifestation of peoples cooperation in;

  1. Strengthening their moral and social - cultural values;
  2. Improving their economic condition thru self-reliance and resiliency;
  3. Alleviating illiteracy;
  4. Strengthening health care services.
  5. Sustaining peace, order and security and the community; and
  6. Maintaining a sustainable for all.









Barangay Cararayan as an Institution with an Utmost service in Education, Health Services, Infrastructure. Children and Youth Development, Social Services and Environmental Consciousness. Through its good, transparent, fair and strong governance that can elevate the economic status of its constituents as socially responsible, family oriented and God- fearing individuals ready and adaptable to the fast changing world of technological advancement and Global Competitiveness.
